Temperature Sensor (DS18B20)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Pin Number |  Pin Name  |  Description   |  Wire Color  |  Ardunio Pin Name  | Ardunio Nano Pin Number |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1          | GND        | Ground         | Black        | GND                | Any GND Pin (4, 29)     | 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2          | DATA       | Sensor Data    | Yellow       | D7                 | 10                      |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3          | VCC        | Sensor Power   | Red          | +5V                | 17                      |
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
NOTE: Requires a 4.7K resister between the VCC and DATA pins.
